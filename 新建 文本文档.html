Home

Welcome to the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) Zhengzhou Chapter. Our chapter is dedicated to advancing computational intelligence research, strengthening collaboration between academia and industry, and promoting innovation in artificial intelligence, data science, and intelligent systems across Zhengzhou and the central China region.

Foundation History

The Zhengzhou Chapter of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society began its establishment process in September 2023 and was officially approved in September 2024.

Purpose of Establishment

The chapter aims to provide a platform for academic exchange and intellectual collaboration in Henan Province, promoting disciplinary development and fostering innovation in the field of computational intelligence.

Leading Institutions

The chapter is jointly supported by Zhengzhou University, Zhongyuan University of Technology, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Henan Normal University, and other academic organizations.

About Us

About IEEE CIS

The IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) is a professional society of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) dedicated to advancing the theory, design, application, and development of computational intelligence paradigms.

About Zhengzhou

Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province, is a historical and cultural hub located at the heart of China and a growing center for intelligent technologies.

Our Mission

  • Strengthen partnerships with universities and research institutes
  • Promote academic exchange and international collaboration
  • Support young researchers and talent development
