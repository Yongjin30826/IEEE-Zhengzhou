Welcome to the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) Zhengzhou Chapter. Our chapter is dedicated to advancing computational intelligence research, strengthening collaboration between academia and industry, and promoting innovation in artificial intelligence, data science, and intelligent systems across Zhengzhou and the central China region.

Foundation History

The Zhengzhou Chapter of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society began its establishment process in September 2023 and was officially approved in September 2024.

Purpose of Establishment

The chapter aims to provide a platform for academic exchange and intellectual collaboration in Henan Province, promoting disciplinary development and fostering innovation in the field of computational intelligence.

Leading Institutions

The chapter is jointly supported by Zhengzhou University, Zhongyuan University of Technology, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Henan Normal University, and other academic organizations.

About Us

About IEEE CIS

The IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) is a professional society of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) dedicated to advancing the theory, design, application, and development of computational intelligence paradigms. The society focuses on neural networks, evolutionary computation, fuzzy systems, swarm intelligence, and hybrid intelligent systems inspired by biological, linguistic, and social processes. IEEE CIS promotes scientific exchange, technological innovation, and global collaboration across academia, industry, and research communities.

About Zhengzhou

Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province, is a historical and cultural hub located at the heart of China. As one of the country’s national central cities and major transportation hubs, Zhengzhou plays a key role in connecting China’s north–south and east–west economic corridors. In recent years, Zhengzhou has rapidly developed in the fields of artificial intelligence, big data, intelligent manufacturing, and electronic information technology. With a growing cluster of universities, research institutions, and high-tech enterprises, Zhengzhou is becoming an important innovation center in central China.

Our Missions / Goals

  • To strengthen partnerships with universities and research institutes in Henan Province and foster talent development in computational intelligence.
  • To promote academic exchange, workshops, seminars, and international collaboration in computational intelligence research.
  • To support young researchers, students, and professionals in joining the global CIS community and contributing to the advancement of intelligent technologies.

Join Us

Whether you are a student, an early-career researcher, or an experienced professional, we welcome you to join the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society. Becoming a member of the IEEE CIS Zhengzhou Chapter allows you to participate in academic events, engage with international experts, and contribute to the growth of computational intelligence in the central China region.
